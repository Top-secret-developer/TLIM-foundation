Volunteer with The Lord is My Shepherd Foundation

What roles will I play as a volunteer?

A TLIMS Foundation volunteer is someone who willingly offers their time, skills, and effort to help improve lives — without expecting financial reward. We welcome anyone who is passionate about community service, empowerment, education, and healthcare to join us in creating real impact. If you are assigned to one of our empowerment or learning centers.

you will work closely with the lead team to support daily activities, ensuring smooth operations and positive experiences for participants. When volunteering during an outreach program, you are expected to arrive on time and will be briefed on your role — whether it involves providing educational support, assisting in medical outreach, or contributing to empowerment initiatives. If you are a skilled volunteer, you’ll be placed in a position that aligns with your area of expertise. Your experience and knowledge will help strengthen our programs and advance our mission of building stronger, healthier, and more empowered communities..
