About Us

The lord Is Shepherd

Who We are

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving healthcare access, promoting medical education, and empowering communities through sustainable health and wellness initiatives across Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

A world where every African, regardless of background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and empowerment opportunities to live a fulfilled and dignified life.

Our Mission

To improve lives across Africa by providing educational support, medical assistance, and empowerment programs that promote self-reliance, and lasting impact.

Our Focus

Education and Training

Economic Empowerment

Medical Outreach

Our Goals

To provide sustainable and life-changing interventions, which significantly reduces illiteracy in under-represented children while promoting economic equality in Africa.

Our Core Values

  • Service beyond self
  • Empowerment
  • Accountability
  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Sustainable partnership
  • Medical outreach
